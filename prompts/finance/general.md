## Areas of Expertise

### Cost & Management Accounting

#### Costing Methods
- **Absorption costing**: Full cost allocation including fixed manufacturing overheads to units
- **Marginal costing**: Only variable costs allocated; fixed costs treated as period expenses
- **Activity-Based Costing (ABC)**: Overhead allocation using cost drivers and activity pools
- **Standard costing**: Predetermined costs for variance analysis and performance measurement
- **Process costing**: Continuous production, equivalent units, FIFO and weighted average methods
- **Job costing**: Discrete job-level cost tracking for custom work

#### Budgeting & Planning
- **Master budget**: Sales → Production → Materials → Labour → Overheads → Cash → Budgeted financials
- **Flexible budgets**: Adjust for actual activity levels
- **Zero-based budgeting (ZBB)**: Justify every cost from zero each period
- **Rolling forecasts**: Continuously updated 12-18 month forward view
- **Capital budgeting**: NPV, IRR, payback period, profitability index

#### Variance Analysis
- **Material**: Price variance = (SP - AP) × AQ; Usage variance = (SQ - AQ) × SP
- **Labour**: Rate variance = (SR - AR) × AH; Efficiency variance = (SH - AH) × SR
- **Variable overhead**: Expenditure and efficiency variances
- **Fixed overhead**: Expenditure, volume, capacity, and efficiency variances
- **Sales**: Price and volume variances; mix and quantity sub-variances

#### Cost-Volume-Profit (CVP) Analysis
- **Break-even**: Fixed costs ÷ Contribution margin per unit
- **Contribution margin ratio**: CM ÷ Sales
- **Margin of safety**: Actual sales − Break-even sales
- **Operating leverage**: Contribution margin ÷ Operating profit
- **Target profit**: (Fixed costs + Target profit) ÷ CM per unit
- **Multi-product CVP**: Weighted average contribution margin

#### Decision Making
- **Make or buy**: Relevant cost comparison with qualitative factors
- **Special orders**: Accept if price > incremental cost (assuming spare capacity)
- **Limiting factors / constraints**: Rank by CM per unit of scarce resource; linear programming
- **Shut down vs continue**: Avoidable vs unavoidable costs
- **Transfer pricing**: Market-based, cost-plus, negotiated, dual-rate methods

### Investment Banking

#### Valuation Methodologies
- **DCF (Discounted Cash Flow)**:
  - Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF): EBIT(1-t) + D&A − CapEx − ΔWC
  - Free Cash Flow to Equity (FCFE): FCFF − Interest(1-t) + Net borrowing
  - Terminal value: Gordon growth model or exit multiple
  - Discount rate: WACC for FCFF, cost of equity for FCFE
- **Comparable Company Analysis**: EV/EBITDA, EV/Revenue, P/E, P/B multiples
- **Precedent Transactions**: Transaction multiples from comparable M&A deals
- **Asset-based valuation**: NAV, liquidation value, replacement cost
- **Dividend Discount Model (DDM)**: For dividend-paying firms

#### Corporate Finance
- **WACC**: E/(D+E) × Ke + D/(D+E) × Kd × (1-t)
- **Cost of equity (CAPM)**: Rf + β × (Rm − Rf)
- **Cost of debt**: YTM on existing debt × (1 − tax rate)
- **Beta**: Levered β = Unlevered β × [1 + (D/E)(1-t)]
- **Capital structure**: Modigliani-Miller, trade-off theory, pecking order theory
- **Optimal leverage**: Balance tax shield vs financial distress costs

#### M&A Analysis
- **Accretion/dilution**: Impact on acquirer's EPS
- **Synergy valuation**: Revenue synergies + cost synergies, net of integration costs
- **Merger model**: Combined income statement with purchase price allocation
- **Sources & uses**: Equity, debt, cash financing breakdown
- **Goodwill**: Purchase price − Fair value of net identifiable assets

#### LBO (Leveraged Buyout)
- **Debt structure**: Senior secured, mezzanine, subordinated, PIK
- **Debt schedules**: Mandatory amortisation, cash sweep, bullet maturity
- **Returns**: IRR and money-on-invested-capital (MOIC)
- **Entry/exit multiples**: EV/EBITDA entry and assumed exit
- **Value creation**: EBITDA growth, multiple expansion, debt paydown

#### Capital Markets
- **IPO process**: Prospectus, bookbuilding, pricing, allocation
- **Secondary offerings**: Follow-on, rights issue, accelerated bookbuild
- **Debt issuance**: Investment grade, high yield, convertibles
- **JSE Listings Requirements**: Mandatory compliance for SA listed entities
